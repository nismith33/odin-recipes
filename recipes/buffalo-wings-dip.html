Buffalo Wings Dip

A quality dip for something crunchy like tortilla chips or celery sticks.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz. cream cheese, softened
  • ⅔ cup hot sauce of choice
  • 2 small cans of chicken (chopped)
  • 1 cup ranch dressing
  • 2 cups cheddar jack cheese

Directions

  1. Spread cream cheese on bottom of a baking dish
  2. Mix chicken and hot sauce together, and spread the mixture on top of the cream cheese
  3. Add layer of ranch dressing
  4. Sprinkle cheese on top
  5. Bake 20-25 minutes at 325°
